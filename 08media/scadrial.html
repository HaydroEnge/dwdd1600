Scadrial

Intro to Scadrial

Allow me to introduce you to the fascinating world of Scadrial. This remarkable planet is a land of contrasts, where ancient traditions and modern innovations coexist. Scadrial is renowned for its unique magic systems—Allomancy, Feruchemy, and Hemalurgy—which shape the lives and destinies of its inhabitants. The bustling city of Luthadel stands as a testament to human ingenuity, while the mist-covered landscapes evoke a sense of mystery and wonder. As you explore Scadrial, you'll encounter diverse cultures, from the noble houses vying for power to the resilient skaa who toil under their rule. Prepare yourself for an unforgettable journey through a world where metal and magic intertwine, creating a rich tapestry of intrigue and adventure.


Scadrial is home to three distinct and fascinating magic systems: Allomancy, Feruchemy, and Hemalurgy. Allomancy allows individuals to ingest and "burn" metals to gain various abilities, such as enhanced strength, speed, or the power to manipulate emotions. Feruchemy involves storing attributes like health, strength, or speed in metal objects, which can later be tapped to access these stored abilities. Hemalurgy, the darkest of the three, involves transferring powers from one person to another through the use of metal spikes, often at a great cost. Each of these systems is deeply intertwined with the culture and history of Scadrial, creating a world where metal and magic are inextricably linked.
