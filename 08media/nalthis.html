Nalthis

Intro to Nalthis

Welcome to Nalthis, a vibrant and enchanting world within Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere universe. Known for its unique magic system, BioChromatic Breath, Nalthis is a place where color and life intertwine in extraordinary ways. The capital city, Hallandren, is a bustling hub of activity, renowned for its stunning dyes and fabrics, and the presence of the Returnedâ€”deities who have come back from the dead. As you explore Nalthis, you'll discover a rich cultural heritage, intricate magic, and a society where beauty and power are deeply connected.


Nalthis's magic system, known as BioChromatic Breath, is a fascinating and unique aspect of this vibrant world. Each person possesses a Breath, which can be transferred to others, enhancing their senses and abilities. When accumulated, multiple Breaths can animate objects and create powerful beings known as Awakened. The magic is deeply intertwined with color, and practitioners use commands to manipulate the world around them. This system allows for a wide range of magical effects, from simple animations to complex creations, making Nalthis a place where beauty and power are intricately connected.
