Roshar

Intro to Roshar

Allow me to introduce you to the wondrous and tempestuous world of Roshar. This remarkable planet is shaped by relentless highstorms, which carve the landscape and dictate the rhythm of life. The flora and fauna here have evolved to withstand these fierce tempests, resulting in a resilient and diverse ecosystem. As you traverse Roshar, you'll encounter a myriad of cultures, from the warlike Alethi to the enigmatic Parshendi and the reclusive Shin. Each society boasts its own unique customs and traditions, adding to the rich tapestry of this world. Prepare yourself for an unforgettable journey through a land where the very storms breathe life into the fabric of existence.

Among the many wonders of Roshar, none are as legendary as the Knights Radiant. These ten ancient orders of warriors and scholars once protected humanity with their mystical Shardblades and Shardplates, drawing upon the power of spren to perform incredible feats.

Nations of Roshar

Roshar is a world of diverse and vibrant nations, each with its own unique culture and history. The most prominent nation is Alethkar, known for its martial prowess and the Alethi people's warrior ethos. To the west lies Jah Keved, a land of scholars and artists, renowned for its intricate fabrial technology. Shinovar, isolated by mountains, is home to the peaceful and reclusive Shin people, who follow a strict code of honor. The Reshi Isles are a collection of floating islands inhabited by a seafaring people with a deep connection to the ocean. Azir, the heart of the Azish Empire, is a bureaucratic powerhouse with a complex system of governance. Thaylenah, known for its skilled merchants and sailors, boasts a rich maritime tradition. Herdaz, a small but resilient nation, is famous for its hardy warriors and strategic location. Each of these nations contributes to the rich tapestry of Roshar, creating a world teeming with intrigue, conflict, and cultural diversity.
