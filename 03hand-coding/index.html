Meet the Author

Brandon Sanderson

Brandon Sanderson is a renowned American fantasy and science fiction author, best known for his intricate world-building and compelling storytelling. He gained widespread acclaim with his debut novel, Elantris, and further solidified his reputation with the Mistborn series. Sanderson was also chosen to complete Robert Jordan's epic The Wheel of Time series, a testament to his skill and dedication to the genre. His works often explore themes of power, morality, and the human condition, making him a beloved figure in contemporary fantasy literature. Brandon also started his own publishing company, DragonSteel Entertainment.

One of Sanderson's most ambitious projects is the creation of the Cosmere, a shared universe where many of his novels take place. This interconnected universe includes series such as Elantris, Mistborn, Warbreaker, and The Stormlight Archive. The Cosmere is characterized by a single creation myth and a unified system of magic, which Sanderson meticulously developed to provide a cohesive framework for his stories. This allows characters and elements from different series to interact, enriching the reader's experience with subtle crossovers and deeper lore.

Worlds of the Cosmere

Roshar
The setting of The Stormlight Archive, known for its highstorms and magical spren.
Scadrial
The backdrop for the Mistborn series, where Allomancy, Feruchemy, and Hemalurgy shape society.
Nalthis
Featured in Warbreaker, where the magic system revolves around BioChromatic Breath.
Sel
The setting of Elantris and The Emperor's Soul, with its complex system of AonDor.
Taldain
From White Sand, where the magic is influenced by the planet's unique solar cycles.
