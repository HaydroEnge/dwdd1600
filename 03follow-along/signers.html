Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration and third President of the United States.
John Adams
Advocate for independence and second President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned polymath, diplomat, and inventor.
John Hancock
President of the Second Continental Congress, known for his large and bold signature.
Samuel Adams
Leader in the American Revolution and cousin of John Adams.

These individuals played significant roles in the founding of the United States and are well-remembered for their contributions.
