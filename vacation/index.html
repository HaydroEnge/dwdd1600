Our Story

Founded in 2009, Sakura Trails began as a small team of Kyoto enthusiasts passionate about sharing the hidden beauty of Japan's cultural heart. Over the past decade, we’ve grown into one of the region’s most trusted boutique travel agencies. We specialize in immersive, authentic, and deeply personalized experiences.

Who We Serve

We welcome solo travelers, couples, families, and small groups seeking a deeper connection with Kyoto's history, cuisine, nature, and spirituality. Whether you’re planning a romantic getaway or a cultural deep dive, our itineraries are designed with care, elegance, and attention to detail.
